Protect Our Water, Protect Our Future

Water pollution is one of the biggest threats to our environment and health. Learn how you can make a difference today.

The Silent Crisis

Every day, millions of tons of industrial waste, agricultural runoff, and plastic debris enter our water bodies. This not only harms marine life but also contaminates the water we drink.

Polluted water transmits diseases like cholera, dysentery, typhoid, and polio. Contaminated drinking water is estimated to cause 485,000 diarrhoeal deaths each year.

How You Can Help

Don't Dump Waste

Never dispose of household chemicals, oil, or plastic in drains or rivers. They end up in our water supply.

Conserve Water

Use water wisely. Less water usage means less wastewater that needs treatment.

Participate in Cleanups

Join local community cleanup drives. Every piece of trash removed makes a difference.

How to Report Effectively

1. Capture Evidence

Take clear photos or videos of the dumping site. Ensure the location is visible.

2. Note Details

Note the exact location, time, and type of waste (industrial, plastic, etc.).

3. Submit Report

Use the links above or our app to submit. Provide as much detail as possible.

Simple Actions, Big Impact

  • Say No to Single-Use Plastics

    Carry a reusable water bottle and shopping bag. Plastic waste is a major water pollutant.

  • Recycle Properly

    Ensure you separate your waste. Recyclables that end up in landfills often leach into groundwater.

  • Report Pollution

    Use DumpDetect to report illegal dumping or pollution spots. Your vigilance can save a river.
